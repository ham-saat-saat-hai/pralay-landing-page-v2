Anushka is currently a doctoral student of psychology at Vanderbilt University. Centred around social-cognitive science, her research focuses on what people believe to be true, how they form beliefs, and ways in which interventions can encourage discernment and accuracy. With Pralay, she hopes to create an exciting and rich narrative experience that leads players to think more reflexively about the world around them.
