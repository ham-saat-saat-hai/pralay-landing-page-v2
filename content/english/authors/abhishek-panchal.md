Abhishek is a research scientist at Ashoka University. His current research focuses on modeling diseases spread using Agent Based Simulation and coupling them with GIS and infrastructure. He was previously a weather scientist at the Department of Space and has experience working with weather and climate models on high performance computing systems. Interested in speculative  narrative driven stories using realistic climate scenarios and Game Theory, he aims to engage people in climate science without the alarmist approach.
